# DESIGN

## Style Prompt

Use a midnight editorial audit-desk style: warm paper-gold typography on deep navy-black, translucent glass cards, thin ledger-grid lines, and restrained amber/red signal glows. The video should feel like a clear thinking instrument, not a motivational poster. The mood is calm, forensic, and slightly cinematic.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#07090f`
- Deep panel: `#101722`
- Primary text: `#efe2c7`
- Highlight gold: `#e7a93d`
- Warning clay: `#c7643b`
- Soft steel: `#8ea7b4`

## Typography

- Serif headline: `Noto Serif SC`, fallback `Songti SC`
- Sans UI: `Noto Sans SC`, fallback `PingFang SC`

## Motion

- Use slow audit-table reveals, card stacking, and precise line sweeps.
- Keep movement meaningful: facts appear first, story labels arrive second, action arrows arrive third.
- Avoid frenetic kinetic typography; this is a thinking-tool video.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use purple gradients or generic tech neon.
- Do not make the video look like a motivational quote slideshow.
- Do not use cosmic stars for the “universe” title; the usable tool is narrative audit.
- Do not overload one screen with more than one main idea.
